1) Summarize the Apollodorus reading in less than 100 words

	The Apollodorus reading quickly tells the story of how Zues came to power by defeating his father Cronos who previously defeated his own father Ouranos. The reading also explains the lineage of many immortal beings and of who each were born from.

2) Summarize the Hesiod reading in less than 100 words.

	The Hesiod reading is a poem, so the story is told in a very different manner. The content of the story is about the creation of the Gods and how Zues became the leader of them all by defeating his predecessor and father, Cronos with the help of his allies.
